Our Story

Founded in 1985 by John and Maria Thompson, Tiles & Hardware Emporium began as a small family-owned store with a big dream: to provide the finest quality tiles and hardware to our local community. From those humble beginnings, we've grown into a respected industry leader, serving homeowners, contractors, and designers across the nation.

Our journey has been marked by a relentless pursuit of excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction. As we've expanded our product range and services, we've never lost sight of our core values: quality, integrity, and personalized service.

Today, we're proud to offer an extensive collection of tiles and hardware that cater to diverse styles and needs, from classic elegance to cutting-edge modern designs. Our commitment to sourcing the best products from around the world ensures that our customers always have access to the latest trends and highest quality materials.
